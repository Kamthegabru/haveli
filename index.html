Old-world charm meets modern Portland. Our kitchen celebrates North Indian flavors — from charcoal-kissed kebabs to slow-simmered curries — crafted with care and spice.

Our Story

A Heritage of Hospitality

In the spirit of a traditional haveli—a home built for gatherings—we bring people together through food. Our journey began with a simple vision: to create a space in Portland that felt like coming home.

Our recipes are heirlooms, passed down through generations, preserving the authentic taste of North India. From our fiery tandoor to our slow-simmered curries, each dish is crafted with passion, patience, and the finest local ingredients.
